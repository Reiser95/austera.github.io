What is Mason

Meson is The first perfect gambling token to give fair rewards to holders based on their commitment to Mason.

We provide a tiered gambling system, the higher tier you're in, the more rewards, you get. The tier is being determined based on your purchased ETH amount and your GSHIBA holdings.

Masonry NFT marketplace

We'll release Mason NFTs.
