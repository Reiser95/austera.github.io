About Us

What is Gambler Shiba?

Gambler Shiba (GSHIBA) is The first perfect gambling token to give fair rewards to holders based on their commitment to GSHIBA. We provide a tiered gambling system, the higher tier you're in, the more rewards, you get. The tier is being determined based on your purchased ETH amount and your GSHIBA holdings.

GSHIBA is the real game-changer in crypto tier systems, as we calculate the tier based on the purchased ETH amount, not only by the GSHIBA holdings.
