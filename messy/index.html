The messy cats

Were living their best life in an art gallery doing what they loved most: whale hunting, eating leftovers, plotting world domination and recreating their favorite hooman paintings.

Hey at least they weren’t scratching them.

After a CATastrophe tragically destroyed the museum these fascinating felines were forced to hit the streets to find a puuurfect new home.

Messy Cats is a collection of 5,048 adorable hand-drawn unique NFTs roaming the Ethereum Blockchain.

Each kitty is waiting for a new home in a hot and cozy wallet to be adopted into.
