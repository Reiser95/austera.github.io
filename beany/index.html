Beany Coin (BEANY)

Here at Beany.DeFi we want to build the best community of animal lovers in the world. To protect our beloved community we have added some anti-whale measures, to punish anyone who tries selling for quick turn arounds. In doing so, they will incur a total selling fee of 9%.

These funds will then be allocated as following automatically: 4% reflection rewards for holders. 2% Allocated to LP, this will help stabilise and increase market liquidity. 3% to be used to Buy Beany Coin of the market and send to an address which can't be accessed thus reducing supply and increasing scarcity over time. Our buy fee is set at 3% and will be allocated as a 2% reflection reward for holders, and 1% to charity.
